Green Grow

Sustainablity at Ease

  • An automated framework for Soil Profiling, Calculating the soil degradation and thereby recommending crop and fertilizer to aid in sustainable farming.

  • Green Grow uses geospatial data to promote the sustainability at farmer's end

  • The framework consists of machine learning models to enable accurate predictions.

  • This framework aims to predict the appropriate fertilizer and crop based on the soil state at a particular region.

  • Green Grow acts as a one-stop solution for facilitating sustainable farming in a easy to understand format.
